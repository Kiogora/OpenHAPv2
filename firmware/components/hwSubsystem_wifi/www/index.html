Overall steps

Step 1. Setup the device physically in the household.

Step 2. Connect to WIFI network OpenHAP-XX:XX:XX:XX:XX:XX where XX:XX:XX:XX:XX:XX is the Device identification

Step 3. After successful connection, go to 192.168.4.1 on chrome browser

Step 4. Check the thermal camera, device time and pollution level on the View sensors link above.The page takes time to load

Step 5. Check the wriststrap signal on the View wristband link above

Step 6. If all is okay, start the measurement by clicking Start measurement link above

Step 7. Leave the device for 2-3 days

Step 8. Retrieve the device and do note the retrieval time

Step 9. Power up the device and retrieve the recorded data as below

Step 10. On android, long press the file on this webpage and choose to download to device.

Step 11. Share the file to the whatsapp group.

Step 12. After supervisor approval, delete measurement(s) from device to save space in preparation for next measurement and repeat steps.
